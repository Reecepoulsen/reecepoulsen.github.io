Notes

  • Master Mobile UX Videos
    • Always design mobile first! It helps you narrow down on what content is most important for your site
    • Provide users with what they want to see as soon as possible
    • Reduce steps!
    • Ways to improve loading times: reduce image usage, use svg or webfonts, optimize and minify css and js files, and gzip your files.
    • Keep important buttons within reach for your user. Will give the appearance of a faster site because they won't have to reach as far to interact.
