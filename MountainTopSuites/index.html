Temple Spotlight: Idaho Falls, Idaho

Dedicated on September 23rd, 1945, the Idaho Falls temple sits beautifully along the snake river.

Having been one of the first buildings of significant size in the area, a strong community has grown around the temple. From the peaceful nature path across from the temple to the restaurants and stores nearby, there is plenty to do and see!
