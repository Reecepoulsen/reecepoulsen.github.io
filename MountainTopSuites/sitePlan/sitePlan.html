Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Mountain Top Suites site is designed to attract visitors and infor them of the services that are offered. The end goal is to convince the visitor of the worth of these hotels and encourage them to stay at one of the locations.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site consists of temple workers, patrons and their families, and people who might not even be members of the church. Ideally, these people are looking for a place to stay near a temple and if not, they are thinking about it.
