Chords That Bind

We all have moments in life when we wish to share our feelings with someone else in a way that goes deeper than words. Some turn to poetry, others turn to art, and the rest of us turn to music. Music is meant to be shared, and when it is, connections happen. Imagine a place where you can share the music that tells your story. Chords aims to make that place a reality. My vision is to create a platform in which you can connect with your favorite people through the chords of your favorite music.

The Vision

Chords is a social media platform designed around celebrating the art of music and its ability to bring people together. Similar to other social media platforms, each person will have a profile to represent themself and their taste in music. Chords isn't meant to replace your favorite music streaming app, in fact, it is designed to work hand in hand with the streaming app of your choice. Once connected, all of your playlists will be automatically imported to your Chords account and then you can start building your profile.

What to Expect

Obviously, this idea is way too big for a student in their first web development class, but when I stumbled upon it, I realized that its never been done before! Music and programming are two of my passions, so I figured that I would turn this assignment into something that I could be passionate about. The purpose of this website is to help you catch the vision. The other two associated pages demonstrate what a profile could look like and some of the features I would like to see included in the application. There is also an example of a login page.
