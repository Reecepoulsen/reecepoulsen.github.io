Overview

Purpose

We love our music! The average person spends about 2.6 hours a day listening to music, that’s a whole 18 hours a week! But, if you're like me, your playlist can start to feel stale and repetitive with all that listening. If only there was a way to discover new music more frequently. That’s where my app, Chords, comes in. Chords is a social media platform based on the idea of sharing the music that you listen to with your friends. Chords combines two modern ideas: Social Media and music streaming to build a profile that can help you discover the music your friends listen to and suggest new music you might like.

Audience

My target audiences for this website are those who love listening to and discovering new music. Social media has become infused into each of our lives and Chords aims to create a link between our love for being connected with others and our love for listening to music. This website hopes to fill the niche for music lovers that instagram fills for picture lovers.

Chords That Bind

We all have moments in life when we wish to share our emotions with someone else in a way that goes deeper than words. Some turn to poetry, others turn to art, and the rest of us turn to music. Music is meant to be shared, and when it is, connections happen. Imagine a place where you can share the music that tells your story. Chords aims to make that place a reality. My vision is to create a platform in which you can connect with your favorite people through the chords of your favorite music.

The Vision

Chords is a social media platform designed around celebrating the art of music and its ability to bring people together. Similar to other social media platforms, each person will have a profile to represent themself and their taste in music. Chords isn't meant to replace your favorite music streaming app, in fact, it is designed to work hand in hand with the streaming app of your choice. Once connected, all of your playlists will be automatically imported to your Chords account and then you can start building your profile.

What to Expect

Obviously, this idea is way too big for a student in their first web development class, but when I stumbled upon it, I realized that its never been done before! Music and programming are two of my passions, so I figured that I would turn this assignment into something that I could be passionate about. The purpose of this website is to help you catch the vision. The other two associated pages demonstrate what a profile could look like and some of the features I would like to see included in the application.

My Profile

A profile would include a heading that would have a profile picture and a banner image behind the profile picture. Underneath that, there would be a nav bar to toggle between different pages. The body of the profile would contain a couple of widgets. The first would be a section to display your top 3 artist picks and your top 5 songs from each artist. The second would include your top 25 songs. And the third would be your 10 most recently added songs.

Features

This section contains a list of features and their descriptions:

  • Friends

    This feature would be a mix between a feed and a messaging system. This is where you would interact with your friends, share new songs that you found, and visit friend's pages to see their favorites.

  • Genres

    This feature will automatically organize your music into genres. I've always found it difficult to relate to a genre because I have no idea what genres the artists I listen to are a part of, this section should solve that problem.

  • Discover

    Similar to Spotify, I want to include a way for those looking for new music to discover their next favorites. This section would include something similar to Spotify's discover weekly. I would also add 2 other features, the first would suggest new songs based off of the genres you listen to the most and the second would recommend new music based off of what your friends are listening to.

  • Activites

    We all have music that we listen to specifically when we are working out, doing homework, relaxing, riding in the car, and just about anything else you can think of. The idea behind an activity is similar to a hashtag, it would be a way of connecting a song to an activity. Then you could browse through the activity top charts and see what other people listen to.

  • Lyrics

    I love learning the lyrics behind songs but I don't like having to hop onto google to look them up. Chords will have an in-app capability to display lyrics.

  • Behind The Scenes

    I feel like every song has a story to tell but sometimes it can be hard to tell what that story is. This feature would allow you to look up the "behind the scenes" information for each song so you can finally figure out why your favorite song was written.
