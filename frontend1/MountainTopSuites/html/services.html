Services

Changing Rooms

Changing rooms are available to all who come visit the temple, no purchase necessary. Changing rooms include privacy stalls and lockers that come in individual and family sizes.

Playroom

Short-term babysitting is offered in our playroom at a low, reasonable cost. Leave your kids with us while you go and serve in the house of the Lord.

Temple Theme

Each of our locations includes a uniquely themed sitting room that is based off of the corresponding temple.

Family Search Center

A fully staffed family history center is also included for each guest. Need to print off your ordinance card? We've got you covered with an ordinance card printing center.

Reception Hall

Special arrangements can be made for those who would like to reserve our reception hall, guest or not. Click on the button below to learn more!

Looking to Reserve the Reception Hall?

Let us help make the most important day of your life easier!

Our reception hall comes stocked with tables and chairs that can be moved around as you please. You can also take your pick from our menu for food at your event.

Fill out the form on the 'Contact' page and we will reach out to you to discuss any questions you may have!

Temple Missionary Rooms

Special rooms are available for those that are serving as full-time temple missionaries. At an affordable, long-term cost. Click the link below to learn more!

Looking for a Place to Stay as a Temple Missionary?

Full-time temple missionaries have the option to rent out one of our kitchenette suites at an affordable price.

You help keep the temple running, this is our way of giving back!

Fill out the form on the 'Contact' page and we will reach out to you to discuss any questions you may have!

Breakfast Included

Breakfast is included for each guest every morning from 6-9am. Come and enjoy some good food!

Exercise Room

A fully equiped exercise room is available for use to all guests. Equipment included for all different age groups and experience levels. Open 24/7.

Indoor Pool

Come take a load off at the indoor pool included with your stay! While the kids swim, you can enjoy an evening in the hot tub!

High-Speed Wifi

Access to high speed internet is included with each reservation. Smart Tvs with streaming capabilities are included in each room.

Temple Shuttle/Parking

Our very own shuttle periodically takes trips to and from the temple. We do our best to line up with the ordinance schedule of the temple to get where you want to go quickly.

Family History Consultation

Want to prepare family names before you go to the temple? Get answers to your questions from our family history consultants.
