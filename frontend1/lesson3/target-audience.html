Target Audience

This site's target audience consists of those who want to know what the weather is currently like and will be like in the near future so that they can dress accordingly. This audience would include all age groups able to use a computer.

Personas

  • 1) Jeff

    Jeff is a college student who lives in Preston Idaho. With all of his classes being switched to zoom, he doesn't get out much. He decided that he was gonna spend more time outside on days that are nice and sunny!

  • 2) Sally

    Sally loves the Napolean Dynamite movie! She has always dreamed of taking a couple of days off so she can take a vacation to visit Napolean's ol' stomping grounds in Preston Idaho. Being from Texas, she has never been to Preston and has no clue what the weather will be like! What should she pack??

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Jeff woke up an hour before class! Normally he rolls out of bed 15 minutes before hand, so this was an early start for him. He was excited to get outside at some point during the day but had no clue what the weather was going to be like. He grabbed his phone so he could check.

  • Scenario 2: Sally is trying to figure out what she needs to pack for a successful 3 day trip to Preston Idaho! Since she is flying, she only has a limited amount of space in her suitcase. She grabs her phone to see if she can get some more info.

  • Scenario 3: Jeff is running late for a party at his best friend's house. He looks out the window and sees that it is pretty cloudy! He wonders if he should grab a jacket just in case.

  • Scenario 4: Sally finally gets to Preston and to her surprise, it was snowing! She had only packed a light jacket because the forecast didn't say it would snow! Wanting to make the most of her trip and see the sites, she wondered how long the snow would last.

  • Scenario 5: Jeff was trying to plan a day to go mountain biking with his friends. He had finally nailed down a time that worked for everyone, but then he realized that he hadn't checked the weather yet. He grabbed his phone, crossing his fingers that it would be a nice day!
